NFL-Hang-Man

How to play

Press any key to start the game. Once the game starts the computer chooses a random word that you have to guess it by choosing letters.

The word to guess is represented by a row of underscores, representing each letter of the word.

To guess a letter you can press the key on your keyboard.

If you choose a letter which occurs in the word, the letter appears in all its correct positions and you wil win++. If the chosen letter does not occur in the word, you loss a live.

When you loss all the lives, the game is over and you can try again.
